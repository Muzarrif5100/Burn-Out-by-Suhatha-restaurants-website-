    Our Menu

    Discover OUR HISTORY

    Burn Out by Suhatha is a Sri Lankan restaurant in Akkaraipattu Embark on a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Sri Lanka. Our chef offers a variety of authentic biryanis, smoky BBQ dishes, and traditional Sri Lankan specialties infused with a modern twist.

    WHAT you will taste at our place :

    • Smoky BBQ Biryani (Chicken, Beef, and Veggie options).
    • Signature Sri Lankan Curries with rice or naan.
    • Tandoor-Grilled Kebabs.
    • Grilled Meats infused with authentic Sri Lankan spices.

      • Choose your biryani base (Long-grain Basmati, Jeera Samba, or Red Rice) and it is served after being infused with smoky, charcoal-grilled meats or vegetables, and topped with our signature blend of Sri Lankan spices.
